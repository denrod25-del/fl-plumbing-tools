Privacy Policy

How we handle your information across email sign-ups and the Get Matched lead form.

Effective date: [effective date] · Last updated: [effective date]

This Privacy Policy explains how [Company/Operator Name] ("FL Plumbing Tools," "we," "us") collects, uses and shares information when you use flplumbingtools.com. Questions: [contact email] or (561) 316-7450.

Information we collect

  • Calculator use. Our calculators run in your browser. The values you enter into a calculator are not required to be submitted to us to get a result.
  • Email sign-up. If you choose to subscribe, we collect the email address you provide. Email sign-ups are processed and stored using Mailchimp (Intuit Mailchimp), acting as our email service provider. Mailchimp processes your data under its own privacy terms.
  • Get Matched lead form. If you request to be matched with a plumber, we collect the contact details and project information you submit (such as name, phone, email, location and a description of your job).
  • Automatically collected data. Like most websites, we and our service providers may collect standard technical data such as IP address, device and browser type, and general usage analytics.

How we use information

  • To provide our tools and respond to your requests.
  • To send emails you have subscribed to (you can unsubscribe at any time via the link in each email).
  • To connect you with a licensed plumber when you use Get Matched.
  • To maintain, secure and improve the site.

Lead sharing disclosure

Important: When you submit the Get Matched form, you are asking us to share your contact and project information with one or more licensed third-party plumbing professionals (and/or a lead-matching partner) so they can contact you about your project. By submitting that form you consent to this sharing. We share lead information only for the purpose of fulfilling your request.

Consent to be contacted (TCPA notice)

By submitting the Get Matched form and providing your phone number, you consent to be contacted by FL Plumbing Tools and/or the matched plumbing professional(s) about your request, including by phone call and text message, which may use automated technology, at the number you provide. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by telling the caller/sender to stop or replying STOP to texts. This notice is provided to address requirements such as the U.S. Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA); [Company] should confirm exact consent language with counsel.

Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies for basic functionality and aggregate analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How we share information

We share information with: service providers who operate the site on our behalf (such as Mailchimp for email and our hosting/analytics providers); plumbing professionals or lead partners when you use Get Matched; and others as required by law or to protect rights and safety. We do not sell your personal information for unrelated third-party advertising.

Your choices and rights

  • Unsubscribe from emails using the link in any message.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting [contact email]. Available rights may depend on your state of residence.
  • Decline to submit the Get Matched form if you do not want your information shared with plumbers.

Data retention and security

We retain personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, and we use reasonable measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children

This site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the effective date above when we do.
