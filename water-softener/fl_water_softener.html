●
Rotten egg smell from hot water (sulfur/H2S)
●
Metallic, salty, or chlorine taste in water
●
Itchy skin or dry hair after showering
●
Soap won't lather well, leaves film on skin
●
Cloudy water or visible particles or sediment

Water heater failing early or making popping sounds
