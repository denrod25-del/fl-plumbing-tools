📏 Toilet Rough-In Distance Calculator

Toilet rough-in distance is measured from the finished wall to the center of the closet flange (drain). This is NOT the toilet-to-wall gap — it's the drain centerline from the wall behind the toilet.

Standard: 12". Older FL homes may have 10" or 14". Measure from wall to drain center.

Leave 0 if measuring to finished wall face. Add 0.5" for tile, 4" for typical stud wall.

📋 FL Plumbing & Accessibility Code
FPC Key Rough-In References

FPC 405.3.1 — Toilet location: Toilet shall be located a minimum of 15" from its centerline to any side wall, partition, or cabinet. This applies to BOTH sides. For ADA: minimum 18" from one side (transfer side).

FPC 405.3.2 — Toilet clearance: Minimum 21" clearance in front of toilet (measured to any obstacle). ADA requires 60" minimum turning radius or 30"×48" clear floor space.

FPC 412.2 — Shower receptors: Shower floor shall slope uniformly to drain at 1/4" per foot minimum. Curb height 2" minimum. Threshold maximum 1/2" for accessibility compliance.

FPC 424.6 — Grab bar blocking: FL requires blocking or solid backing in shower/tub areas of new residential construction to allow future grab bar installation (FBC 11-4.23.4). 2×8 blocking required between studs.

FPC 603.2 — Water heater location: Gas WH in garage requires 18" minimum elevation of ignition source above garage floor. Electric WH may be floor-mounted. Drip pan required for WH in conditioned space or above finished area.

ADA (ADA-ABA Guidelines / FBC Ch. 11)

FL Building Code Chapter 11 adopts the ADA Standards for Accessible Design for all new commercial construction and some residential. Key fixture requirements:

Lavatories (FBC 1109.2): Rim height max 34"; knee clearance min 27" high × 30" wide × 19" deep; clear floor space 30"×48" in front; supply/drain insulated or covered to prevent burns.

Water closets (FBC 1109.4): Height 17"–19" to top of seat; 60" clear floor space side approach OR 66"×56" for front approach; flush control on open side.

Shower stalls (FBC 1109.6): 36"×36" for transfer shower or 30"×60" for roll-in shower; seat required for transfer-type; controls between 15"–48" AFF; hand shower on 60" minimum hose.

Bathtubs (FBC 1109.5): Foot controls at 60" AFF; head controls at 60" AFF max 15" from foot wall; grab bars at 24" front, 12" back, side wall full length.
