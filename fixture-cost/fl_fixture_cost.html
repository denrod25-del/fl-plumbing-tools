⚠️ FL Hard Water Notice
FL hard water (150–350 mg/L) corrodes cartridges and aerators faster than the national average. Ask about hard water-resistant valve cartridges when getting your quote.
ℹ️ Shower Valve Wall Access
Shower valve replacement requires opening the wall in some FL homes. Get a quote to confirm access method and patching/finishing scope before work begins.

💧 FL Water Pressure Impact
FL homes often run at 65–80 PSI. High pressure accelerates faucet wear and can void some manufacturer warranties. Ideal residential pressure: 40–60 PSI. Ask your plumber about a pressure reducing valve (PRV) if you're replacing faucets frequently.
📋 FL Permit Note
Like-for-like faucet, showerhead, and hose bib replacements generally do not require a permit in FL. Shower valve replacements involving opening walls may require a permit in some FL counties — confirm with your contractor before scheduling.
