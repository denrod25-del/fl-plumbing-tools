5 FL Drain Facts Every Homeowner Must Know
  • 1FL's hard water (200–400 ppm in many areas) deposits calcium scale that narrows pipes by up to 40% over 10 years.
  • 2Live oak, ficus, and palm roots are the top pipe-invaders in FL — roots seek moisture year-round due to our dry seasons.
  • 3Sewer camera inspections are now required for FL home sales in most counties — reveals jetting needs before closing.
  • 4Restaurant grease traps in FL must be pumped every 30–90 days (per local county ordinance) — FOG backs up residential drains too.
  • 5Hydro-jetting uses 3,500–4,000 PSI water pressure — safe for PVC, cast iron, and most FL pipe materials.

🚫 Do Not Use Chemical Drain Cleaners
DO NOT use chemical drain cleaners in FL homes — they corrode PVC fittings and are ineffective on mineral scale. FL's hard water requires mechanical clearing. Chemicals also damage septic systems.
🛡️ Preventive Jetting FL
Annual jetting for homes with mature trees or hard water costs $200–400/year and prevents $3,000+ emergency repairs. Many FL plumbers offer maintenance contracts.
