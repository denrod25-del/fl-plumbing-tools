How our estimates are generated

Each calculator combines Florida-specific cost research with current building-code and permitting references. Content is drafted with AI assistance and reviewed for accuracy by the FL Plumbing Tools editorial team before it is published.

Where the numbers come from

Ranges are built from published Florida contractor pricing, supplier and material costs, and permit-fee schedules from local building departments—then sanity-checked against real-world job examples. They are planning ranges, not quotes, bids or guarantees.

How Florida pricing varies by region

Costs differ across the state—South Florida and the Keys, coastal counties, and inland or rural areas all price differently for labor, permits and materials. Our tools reflect Florida ranges and note where regional factors move the estimate.

Reviewed and kept current

The editorial team reviews cost assumptions and code references on a recurring basis and updates tools as Florida pricing and requirements change. Every page carries a visible last-updated date.

How we select plumber recommendations

When you choose to compare pros, we prioritize Florida plumbers whose licenses can be verified through the state (DBPR / CFC license lookup) and who carry insurance. Verification steps are documented on our vetting page.

Consumer-first by design

Every tool is free with no signup or email, we don't sell you a fixed price, and we publish our methodology and editorial standards openly so you can check our work.
