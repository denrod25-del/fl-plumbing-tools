    Florida Backflow Prevention Law

    FAC 62-555.360 mandates annual testing of all testable backflow prevention assemblies connected to public water systems. Non-compliance can result in water service interruption by your utility.
    Governing Codes & Standards
    FAC 62-555.360 Annual testing mandate (FL DEP)
    FPC §608 FL Plumbing Code backflow section
    FBC §604.8 Cross-connection control
    FS 373.185 Reclaimed water requirements
    FAC 62-610 Reuse of reclaimed water
    ASSE 1013/1015/1020 RPZ / DC / PVB device standards
    AWWA M14 Cross-connection control manual
